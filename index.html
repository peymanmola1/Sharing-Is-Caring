Blues fans will forever be grateful to John Mikel Obi for his imperious performance on the night we were crowned champions of Europe...

‘This is the best night of our lives; the biggest night of Chelsea’s life.’

So said the Blues No12 at the conclusion of the Champions League final in Munich, having finally got his hands on a trophy the club had serious aspirations of winning for the best of a decade. It was, in fact, our 100th Champions League match of the Roman Abramovich era.
